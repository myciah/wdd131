Who is We Need Disguises Cosplay?

We need Disguises Cosplay is a group of cosplayers who love to cosplay! They are four sisters who enjoy playing video games, watching anime and anything geeky. For this, they started to get into cosplay and eventually started entering cosplay contests. They have entered many cosplay contests throughout the years and have even won many of them!
